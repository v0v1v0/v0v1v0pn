storms R Documentation

Storm tracks data

Description

Tropical cyclone tracks through the Atlantic Ocean, Caribbean Sea and Gulf of Mexico from 1995 to 2005. Only "named" storms, those which reached tropical storm status or stronger, are included.

Usage

storms

Format

name

Storm Name

year,month,day

Date of report

hour

Hour of report (0, 6, 12 or 18, in UTC)

lat,long

Location of storm center

pressure

Air pressure at the storm's center (in millibars)

wind

wtorm's maximum sustained wind speed (in knots)

type

Storm classification (Tropical Depression, Tropical Storm, Hurricane, or Extratropical)

seasday

Day of the hurricane season (days since June 1)

Details

The data originated from the National Hurricane Center's archive of Tropical Cyclone Reports (http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/). This dataset was hand-scraped from best track tables in the individual tropical cyclone reports (PDF, HTML and Microsoft Word) by Jon Hobbs.

The Tropical Cyclone Reports had a variety of storm type designations and there appeared to be no consistent naming convention for cyclones that were not hurricanes, tropical depressions, or tropical storms. Many of these designations have been combined into the "Extratropical" category in this dataset.
