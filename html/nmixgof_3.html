nmixgof R Documentation

Goodness of fit checks for binomial N-mixture models

Description

The package contains methods to compute overdispersion metrics, randomized quantile residuals, and graphical diagnostics of model fit for binomial N-mixture models fitted using the unmarked package. Details about the checks are given in Knape et al. (2018) and at https://www.biorxiv.org/content/early/2017/09/27/194340.

References

Knape et al. 2018. Sensitivity of binomial N-mixture models to overdispersion: the importance of assessing model fit. Methods in Ecology and Evolution, in press.
