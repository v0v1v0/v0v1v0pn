lhd R Documentation

Local Health Districts of NSW.

Description

The geometries have been simplified with a tolerance of 750 m to reduce the level of detail.

Usage

lhd

Format

An object of class sf (inherits from tbl_df, tbl, data.frame) with 15 rows and 11 columns.

Source

Spatial Services, Department of Customer Service NSW. "MyHospitals_Public", https://portal.spatial.nsw.gov.au/portal/home/item.html?id=78df4a3e987b4e7e8b241ee5bfeee358, accessed 4 May 2023. Republished from NSW Ministry of Health, "Map of local health districts", https://www.health.nsw.gov.au/lhd/Pages/lhd-maps.aspx.
